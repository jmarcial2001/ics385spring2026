## Week 11 PyMongo 
 Script connects to MongoDB, creates/uses the Customer collection, deletes old records, inserts 3 customer records, updates one email and one phone number, queries one customer by last name and another by first name, and finally drops the collection. 


## Week 11 Mongoose
Script connects to local MongoDB and Atlas MongoDB, uses Mongoose schemas and models for Customer, Hotel, and Amenities, deletes old records, inserts 3 customer records, 3 hotel records, and 3 amenities records, queries one hotel by name and one amenities record by pool, and then closes both database connections.
